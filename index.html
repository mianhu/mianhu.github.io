CV

Hi!

My name is Mian Hu (胡勉). I am a PhD student under the supervision of Dr. Anna Mendoza at the Faculty of Education, University of Hong Kong. My research interests include EAP (English for Academic Purposes), Teacher Professional Identity, and Second Language Writing.

I received an MA in TESOL from King’s College London in 2016, and a BA in International Politics from Beijing International Studies University in 2014. I taught Staffordshire University International Foundation Program from September 2016 to July 2017, and EAP at Xi’an Jiaotong-Liverpool University from August 2017 to August 2021.

You can reach me via email: mianhu [at] connect.hku.hk
