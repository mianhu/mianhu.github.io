About me

My name is Mian Hu (胡勉). I am a PhD student under the supervision of Dr. Seyyed-Abdolhamid Mirhosseini and Dr. Anna Mendoza at the Faculty of Education, University of Hong Kong. My research interests include sociolinguistics, critical language pedagogy, English for Academic Purposes, and teacher identity.

I received an MA in TESOL from King’s College London in 2016, and a BA in International Politics from Beijing International Studies University in 2014.

You can reach me via email: mianhu [at] connect.hku.hk
