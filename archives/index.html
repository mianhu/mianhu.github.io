About me

My name is Mian Hu (胡勉). I am a PhD candidate under the supervision of Dr. Seyyed-Abdolhamid Mirhosseini and Dr. Anna Mendoza at the Faculty of Education, University of Hong Kong.

I received an MA in TESOL from King’s College London in 2016, and a BA in International Politics from Beijing International Studies University in 2014. I taught EAP between 2016 to 2021.

My current research focuses on the following topics:

  • Sociolinguistics
  • Critical language pedagogy
  • Language teacher identity
  • Langauge education policy
  • Critical EAP

You can reach me via email: mianhu [at] connect.hku.hk
